##How To Learn Vocabulary##
Develop a vocabulary awareness - what you know, what you don't know, what you need to know right now
1. Plan: Set a goal - Lift.do
    * Create a word searching habit - Learn 5 words everyday
    * Look for new words everywhere...movies, music, etc
2. Get lots of language input
    A. Read Read Read...a lot...more - newspaper, magazine, books, blogs, etc. (fiction/non-fiction) gutenberb/goodreads/etc - 
    B. Start with topics that interest you. Not to hard - not to easy (+1). Great to learn the words in context.
    * at least one book 
    * several magazines
    * blogs and info online
    * Listen to the books [LibriVox]
    * Read the dictionary
    * Read a Thesaurus
    * Increase your reading level - longer and more advanced
3. Study The Words & Internalize them    
    A. Mark words you don't know
    * Guess the meaning
    * reticular activating system
    * look for it elsewhere
    * Look them up in dictionary and confirm meaning   
    * highlight the words in the dictionary too - easy review
    B. study word families - nouns, adjectives, verb, adverb - evasive, evasiveness, evasively, evasion, evade
    * study root, prefixes, suffixes (extra - ordinary -ly) [At least half of the words in the English language are derived from Greek and Latin roots.]
    * learn pronunciation
    * learn spelling
    * visual - assosciate with picture or object
    * Look up the words in a thesaurus
    * Associate and connect with other words
    * use Mnemonic Tricks
4. Create Your Memory Learning Tools
    A. Write them down - Notebook - Create personalized dictionary
    * Write definitions
    * Write example sentences - crazy, funny, bizarre
    B. Create substitution exercises
    C. Create Flashcards
    D. Put up post it notes where applicable
    E. SRS for review
5. Activate - Practice, Practice, Practice - Use them in your speaking and writing
    A. Play word games
    * Cross word
    * Search A Word
    * Hidden Words
    * Scrabble
    * Boggle
    * Cranium
    B. Sign up to receive a word of the day
    * Study - course or lessons
    * Visit wordbuilding sites
    * Freerice.com
    * Dictionary.com - popular words/questions***END OF FILE***///Version 1 - Orignal Brainstorming Draft
